Maanya's Artful Days

Kollam patterns flow from her hand,
Geeta's verses, a spiritual stand.
At Chitti's house, she finds her space,
Calligraphy strokes with gentle grace.

Nights bring rest, days filled with care,
Spotify tunes float through the air.
Google Home, a gift made with love,
Her beauty shines, blessed from above.

To her mother, she speaks of me,
Our bond grows strong, for all to see.
Maanya's world, a canvas bright,
Painted with love, from morn to night.

In every act, in every way,
She crafts a masterpiece each day.
